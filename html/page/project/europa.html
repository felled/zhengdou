特色商业科技体验小镇

Characteristics of commercial experience town of science and technology

项目详情

以文化进行主题氛围营造，集摄影、观赏、互动体验为一体的动感参与性项目。具有不确定性、多元性、开放性、流动性、随意性等特点。随机的游玩路线根据人流量等因素而变化，使游客每次游玩的路线都不一样；途中设置了许多科技互动小项目，让“迷路”的孩子在任何角落发现惊喜；生活环境模拟项目让儿童在游玩的过程中学会生活技能，从而增强孩子的自主学习性；适合家庭互动游玩或释放家长游玩，全程监控，卫生又安全。

Theme in cultural ambience, set photography, ornamental, interactive experi- ence for the integration of dynamic participatory projects.Uncertainty, diversity,openness, fluidity, randomness, etc. Random visit route based on factors such as traffic, make visitors each time for a different route; Set up on the way many interactive small projects of science and technology, make "lost"children found in any corner surprise; Living environment simulation project let chil-dren at play in the process of society life skills, to enhance the autonomous learn-ing of children; Suitable for family interaction for pleasure or release parents play,the whole monitoring, health and safety.
