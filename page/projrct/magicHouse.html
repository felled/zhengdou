惊悚魔幻屋

House of horrors

more
惊悚魔幻屋产品是将恐怖体验为与密室逃脱相结合，运用声光电、感应设备、烟雾设备及全息投影设备与主题故事相结合打造而成的科技体验类鬼屋。多媒体互动及高科技手环解密等技术增加游客体验感，并且灵活高效节约人工成本，不同的主题文化依托增加项目文化内涵，游客多维感光将感受到强烈的刺激。
The haunted house combines the horror experience with escaping from the secret room, using technology of acousto-optic electricity, induction equipment, smoke equipment and holographic projection equipment combined with theme stories to create technology experience haunted house. Multimedia interactive and high-tech hand ring decryption technology Increase the sense of visitors experience, and flexible and efficient labor cost savings, different themes based on culture to increase the cultural connotation of the project, visitors will feel a strong sense of multi-dimensional stimulation.
